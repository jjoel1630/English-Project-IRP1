Experience

1937-1939

Teacher

Black Public School in Marion, Virginia

As a teacher, I taught the young students the core concepts of mathematics. I helped develop curriculum and implement this curriculum to teach the students to the best of my abilities.

1953-1958

Mathematician

NACA West Area Computing

As a mathematician for NACA, I helped by performing complex calculations, read data from the black boxes of planes, and carry out other difficult mathematical tasks.

1958-1986

Aerospace Technologist

NASA Spacecraft Controls Branch

As an aerospace technologist for NASA, I helped calculate trajectories for space flights, plot backup navigation charts for astronauts, calculate launch windows, and verify calculations

1961-1961

Mathematician for Project Mercury

NASA Project Mercury

I helped calculate the trajectories for the space flight in which Alan Shepard, the first American in space, would orbit the Earth.

1969-1969

Mathematician for Apollo 11

NASA Apollo 11

I helped calculate trajectories and verify calculations for the Apollo 11 mission that allowed the first man to walk on the moon.
