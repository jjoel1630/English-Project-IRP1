Experience

1942-1943

Mathematics Teacher

Calvert County

As a teacher at Calvert County, I taught the young students the core concepts of mathematics. I helped develop curriculum and implement this curriculum to teach the students to the best of my abilities.

1943-1951

Bookkeeper

National Catholic Community Center

As a bookkeeper for the National Catholic Community Center, I helped maintain the precious books in the center, and check the books out to people.

1951-1953

Research Mathematician

NACA - Langley Research Center

As a research mathematician for NACA, I helped by performing complex calculations, read data from the black boxes of planes, and carry out other precise mathematical tasks.

1953-1958

Mathematician and Physician

NACA - Supersonic Pressure Tunnel

As a Mathematician and Physician for NACA, I worked on the Supersonic Pressure Tunnel project which studied forces on a model by generating wind at around twice the speed of sound.

1958-1985

Aerospace Engineer

NASA

As an aerospace engineer for NASA, I worked in various divisions such as: the Theoretical Aerodynamics Branch of the Subsonic-Transonic Aerodynamics Division at Langley, the Compressibility Research Division, Full-Scale Research Division, High-Speed Aerodynamics Division, and the Subsonic-Transonic Aerodynamics Division
